# Faction system, slice 1: the Church + the faction framework
*Implementation plan, July 2026. Status: **proposed ‚Äî pending decision.** Distilled from a design session; supersedes the flavor-first sketch in [factions.md](../factions.md) where they conflict. Nothing here is built.*

## Context

First slice of [factions.md](../factions.md), evolved through design discussion well past the doc's flavor-first sketch. Decisions, in order:
1. **Profile + ladder, no signature chain yet** (chain is its own slice; capstone wants architects).
2. **Patron admission gates commissions**: the pool starts empty ‚Äî Chapel (400∆í) admits the Church, each Palazzo (900∆í) installs the next noble house (Medici ‚Üí Strozzi ‚Üí Pazzi, table order), Cathedral (1500∆í) also admits the Church and gates its upper rungs.
3. **A faction banner UI** (Civ 6 diplomacy-banner style): clickable crest per admitted patron ‚Üí card with favor, standing, effects. Overturns factions.md's "no faction panel" non-goal ‚Äî deliberately.
4. **Favor is a real relationship meter** ‚Äî overturns the "no relationship meters" non-negotiable (CLAUDE.md + design principle 4), deliberately. Moves on decisions *and* neglect; no time decay (still banned).
5. **Neglect's supporting cast**: since missing an offer costs favor, arrivals must be unmissable and rare ‚Äî a Civ-trade-request-style arrival popup, offer rate roughly halved, rewards buffed to compensate.
6. **Favor is a 0‚Äì100% scale**: rungs unlock by *being above* favor levels, not by cumulative works; slight negative effects below one threshold, a rare severe tier below a lower one ‚Äî including a one-time denunciation prestige hit, the design's single sanctioned citywide consequence.

The guild requesters get removed (doc-slated). Favor/rung/tier mechanics are **requester-generic** (houses ride the same code free); Church-specific parts: chapel admission, cathedral rung-gate, church title pools. Family taste jitter, seed-shuffled install order, rivalry pairs, chains: future slices.

## Favor model (0‚Äì100 per faction)

- **Persisted `favor: Record<string, number>`**; reads default to `FAVOR_START = 50` (neutral), so admission needs no init event.
- **Moves** (clamped 0‚Äì100): **+8** per completed work (`FAVOR_PER_WORK`); **‚àí5** per declined *or* expired open offer (`FAVOR_SLIGHT`). Nothing else; no drift.
- **Rungs by current level**: ‚â•60 / ‚â•75 / ‚â•90 (`FAVOR_RUNGS`) ‚Üí grandeur `FAVOR_GRANDEUR = [1, 1.3, 1.6, 2]` multiplying duration, florins, prestige of that faction's offers. Church rungs 2‚Äì3 additionally require a standing cathedral (effective rung caps at 1 without; favor itself is untouched).
- **Cooled, favor < 35**: the faction's offers skip half the time (one extra rng draw after the requester pick ‚Äî new draw order pinned in the check) and are forced to rung 0.
- **Affronted, favor < 15**: offers skip 75% of the time, rung 0 ‚Äî near-silence, but every rare offer is the recovery path. **Crossing into affronted fires a one-time denunciation**: ‚àí15 city prestige (`DENOUNCE_PRESTIGE`, clamped at 0) + an alert card ("House Pazzi denounces your patronage"). Re-arms only after favor recovers above 15.
- **Rarity check**: from 50, seven consecutive slights with zero completions to reach affronted ‚Äî rare and deliberate, as specified.
- **Pacing rebalance**: `COMMISSION_OFFER_CHANCE` 0.15 ‚Üí **0.08**/month (~one offer/year); rewards compensate ‚Äî `FLORINS_PER_PRESTIGE` 25 ‚Üí **40**, new `COMMISSION_PRESTIGE_SCALE = 1.5` on minted base prestige (constants.ts "quality ‚âà 1..20" display comment updated). All knobs, all starting points.
